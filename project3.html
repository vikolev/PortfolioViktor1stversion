Group Project Website

Title:"4 THE World"

Date: Started 20th November

Ended: 19th January

Project Description: For the second phase of the Orientational phase, again we were split into groups of four and had the assignment to make a website of our choice which will be helpful for students. Me and my groupmembers (Arthur, Chris and Briahna) decided that we will do an online website with Live Discounts for Eindhoven. It will help international students in the search for deals for Clothes, Electronics and Furnitures. My work for the Project was to make the shop page for Eindhoven and create a way for the categories to open up.
